What can you do with WORD COUNTER ?

  • WordCounter is word and character count tool.
  • Automatically count character(with and without space) ,words ,sentences ,paragraph(with option) and average sentence length
  • Automatically shows you the top 10 keywords and keyword density of the article you are writing.
  • You can also copy and paste a document you have already written into the word counter box and it will display the word count and character numbers for that piece of writing.
  • Provide a search box to search a particular word or character in the document
  • Helps to change character case like upper case, lower case, proper case(Every first character of the word in capital letter) and also vice-versa.
  • Helps to check spelling mistake and grammatically mistake in the document.
